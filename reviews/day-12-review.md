## Review 2: Design Focus

The spark particles are the standout. Orange/yellow sparks on wire connection = electric sparks on a breadboard. The particles have gravity (slight downward pull) and fade over ~300ms. This creates a brief but satisfying reward for every connection made.

The gate pop animation (bounce easing) provides immediate visual feedback on placement. Without it, a gate appearing instantly feels abrupt. With it, the placement feels intentional and responsive.
